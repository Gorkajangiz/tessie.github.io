ABOUT THE INTERNSHIP

Inside one of the Netherlands' leading broadcasters

From September 2025 to January 2026, I interned at KRO-NCRV in Hilversum. This was my first experience working within a major broadcasting organisation, and it gave me invaluable insight into the world of television production. I worked primarily with Adobe Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and After Effects, building on the skills I'd developed during my studies.

The fast-paced environment of a public broadcaster taught me how to work efficiently under tight deadlines. I contributed to a variety of projects, from Christmas videos to radio programme highlights and social media content. I also gained experience in filming and lighting, which added another dimension to my post-production work.

This internship confirmed my passion for editing and storytelling. Working alongside experienced professionals showed me the importance of precision, collaboration, and adaptability. It also gave me the confidence to take on more responsibility and trust my creative instincts.
