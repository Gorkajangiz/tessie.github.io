A storyteller with an eye for detail

Hi, I'm Tessa — a 24-year-old video editor and creative storyteller based in the Netherlands. I'm currently finishing my Associate Degree in Digital Technology & Media at Hogeschool Utrecht in Amersfoort, and I've been building my skills through internships at KRO-NCRV and Studio Bocavista.

My passion for video editing started years ago when I began experimenting with making videos for fun. About three years ago, that interest reignited when I started a (very unprofessional) YouTube channel. That experience taught me the basics of editing, and from there, I knew I wanted to turn this into a career.

"I believe that every frame should feel like it was made with intention — because every moment we capture is a moment someone lived."

My toolkit

I work primarily in DaVinci Resolve — I even have my own Pro license — and I've been expanding my skills in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Photoshop. My internship at Studio Bocavista gave me the chance to apply my Resolve skills in a professional environment, while my time at KRO-NCRV taught me the ins and outs of broadcast production.

Colour grading is one of my favourite parts of the process. There's something incredibly satisfying about taking raw footage and shaping it into something that feels alive and intentional. I also enjoy motion graphics and visual effects work — the more I learn, the more I realise how much there is to discover.

When I'm not editing, I'm usually reading, 3D printing, gaming, or finding new ways to experiment with video. These hobbies keep my creativity fresh and remind me why I love this work in the first place.

Beyond the studio

Outside of video editing, I love getting out with my DJI Mini 3 drone to capture aerial footage, and I've recently started experimenting with photography using my new Sony A7SII. There's something freeing about shooting for no one but yourself — it's where I get to try new things and make mistakes without pressure.

I also maintain a personal YouTube channel where I talk about games I've played. It's not professional by any means, but it's a space where I can be myself and keep my editing skills sharp. I haven't posted in almost a year due to school and internship commitments, but I hope to get back to it soon.

If you're looking for an editor who brings genuine care, technical skill, and a collaborative spirit to every project — I'd love to hear from you. Let's make something memorable together.
