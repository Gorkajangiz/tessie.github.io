ABOUT THIS WORK

Social highlights for Adres Onbekend

Adres Onbekend is a program on Radio 5, broadcast every Sunday between 12:00 and 14:00. The show reunites people who have been searching for each other for a long time. My task was to edit social posts from the highlights of these emotional reunions.

Below are the videos I edited for this project. Each clip captures the heart of the reunion — the anticipation, the recognition, and the joy of finding someone after years apart.

Working on Adres Onbekend taught me how to condense powerful, emotional moments into short, shareable social content that still does justice to the stories being told.
