ABOUT THIS WORK

Animation for the Municipality of The Hague

During my internship, I was asked to create an animation for the Municipality of The Hague in After Effects. I received Adobe Illustrator files and raw audio to work with.

My tasks included:
– Editing the audio
– Keyframing scenes
– Adding subtitles

The final video was uploaded to the official YouTube channel of the Municipality of The Hague.
