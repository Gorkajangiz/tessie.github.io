ABOUT THIS WORK

Crafting social presence for Trimetal

During my internship, I created various social media assets for the paint company Trimetal. All of this content was produced using DaVinci Resolve, optimizing workflows to output high-quality, high-tempo videos tailored for mobile audiences.

The projects ranged from motion graphics for short posts to UGC (User-Generated Content)—which involves taking footage provided by product users and turning it into something engaging, authentic, and native to platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

Working on the Trimetal campaign, I learned the importance of dynamic pacing, screen framing (specifically keeping action and titles safe for 9:16 mobile viewports), and color consistency across different user-submitted formats.
