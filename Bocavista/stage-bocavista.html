ABOUT THE INTERNSHIP

Six months inside a creative studio

From February to June 2026, I interned at Studio Bocavista in Vianen. This was my first opportunity to apply my DaVinci Resolve skills in a professional environment, and it was incredibly rewarding. I was trusted with real client projects from day one, working on everything from social media content to corporate animations.

The studio's collaborative atmosphere pushed me to grow quickly. I learned how to interpret client briefs, work within tight deadlines, and deliver work that meets professional standards. I also expanded my After Effects knowledge significantly, picking up new motion graphics techniques from the senior designers.

By the end of the internship, I had contributed to multiple client-facing projects, including work for the Municipality of The Hague and the paint company Trimetal. This experience solidified my passion for post-production and gave me the confidence to pursue a career in this field.
