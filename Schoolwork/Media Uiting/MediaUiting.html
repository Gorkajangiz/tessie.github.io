Showcasing my media skills

For one of my final assignments within the program, I was tasked with creating a media production that demonstrates my capabilities. I decided to make a video that showcases my editing work and the range of skills I've developed.

– Filming (Sony A7SII and DJI Mini 3)
– Editing (DaVinci Resolve)
– Color grading
– Effects in Fusion

This showreel represents the culmination of my learning journey — combining technical proficiency with creative storytelling. It demonstrates my ability to handle every stage of production, from capturing footage with professional cameras and drones to delivering a polished, visually striking final product.
