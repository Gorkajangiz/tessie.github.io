ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Beer commercial with Unreal Engine

For a school assignment, we had to create a video where we placed a scene in Unreal Engine over a green screen. My group decided to make a beer commercial.

My contribution:
– Direction
– Filming
– Editing

This project was a deep dive into virtual production techniques. We filmed our actors against a green screen, then used Unreal Engine to create the background environment. As director, I guided the creative vision, while my work on camera and in the edit brought it all together into a polished commercial.
