ABOUT THIS PROJECT

TV Show production for school

For a school assignment to round off the year, I had the opportunity to help plan and produce a "TV show" that we as students had to organize from scratch.

My roles for this project:
– Created an opener video in a trailer style
– Served as assistant director behind the scenes
– Appeared as a candidate in the other group's show

This project gave me a well-rounded experience in television production — from creating compelling visual content to directing and even being on the other side of the camera. The trailer-style opener set the tone for the entire show, while my work as assistant director helped me understand the coordination and timing required for a live recording.
