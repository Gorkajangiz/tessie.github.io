ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Talkshow production for Fikss

For one of the later assignments during the school year, we had to prepare a talkshow for a client. In my case, this was for the ICT company Fikss and their software XOLVE.

My tasks for this project:
– Camera plan
– Location visit
– Preparatory work
– Operating the camera focused on the talkshow host

This project challenged me to think beyond just operating the camera — I had to plan the entire shoot, visit the location to assess lighting and angles, prepare all necessary equipment, and then execute the camera work during the actual recording. The focus on the talkshow host required precise framing and smooth operation to keep the conversation engaging and visually dynamic.
