ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Walkman commercial with visual effects

For a school assignment, the goal was to create a short video to practice with visual effects techniques: rotoscoping, chroma keying, camera tracking, and motion tracking. The result is the video shown below.

My contribution:
– Filming
– Editing

This project allowed me to dive deep into advanced post-production techniques. Rotoscoping required frame-by-frame masking, chroma keying involved working with green screen footage, and both camera tracking and motion tracking demanded precision to seamlessly integrate elements into the scene. The Walkman theme gave it a nostalgic, fun vibe that made the technical exercise even more enjoyable.
