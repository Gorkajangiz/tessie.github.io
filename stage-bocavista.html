ABOUT THE INTERNSHIP

Six months inside a world-class studio

During my internship at Studio Bocavista, I had the opportunity to immerse myself in the day-to-day operations of a high-end creative production house. From the very first week, I was trusted with real responsibilities — assisting senior editors on live projects, managing media libraries, and contributing to the early assembly of several commercial cuts.

The studio's approach to storytelling is deeply rooted in collaboration and craft. Every project moves through a rigorous creative review process, and sitting in on those sessions was one of the most formative experiences of my career. I learned how to defend an edit, how to receive notes with grace, and how to keep the audience's experience at the centre of every decision.

By the end of the internship, I had contributed to three client-facing projects and had independently assembled two internal promotional cuts for the studio's own reel. It was here that I first understood what it means to work truly professionally — not just technically well, but with intention and accountability.
