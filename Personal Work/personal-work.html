ABOUT THIS WORK

Work made for no one but the story

Personal work is where I get to experiment without boundaries. It's where I try new techniques, make mistakes, and discover what works. Whether it's photography, YouTube videos, or drone footage, these projects keep my creativity alive and feed back into my professional work.

I recently started shooting photography with my new Sony A7SII, using different lenses and colour-correcting in Adobe Lightroom or DaVinci Resolve. I also have a YouTube channel where I talk about games I've played — it's not professional, but it's fun and helps me stay sharp with editing. And when I want to get outside, I take my DJI Mini 3 drone out to capture whatever catches my eye.

These projects are a reminder of why I love this work. They keep me curious, experimental, and always learning. The skills I develop here — colour grading, pacing, composition — directly translate to my client work, making me a more versatile editor and storyteller.
